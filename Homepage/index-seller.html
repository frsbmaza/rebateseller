Rebateskey Process

The bigger the rebate, the ore claims you will have!

You Create!

Set you offer discount and quantity, add pictures and descriptions, add link to your marketplace and publish

We Promote!

Buyers select your products, purchase directly on Amazon and then claim the rebate using their order ID

You Approve!

Rebates are automatically pre-approved and held for 35 days & seller are given 5 days to approve the rebate to ensure there were no returns or non-purchases.

We Deliver!

35 days latter, we'll automatically mail cheeks to your happy shoppers
